TruePosition Case Study Video

One evening two men went kayaking. Sudden rough weather caused their kayaks to capsize and sink. Luckily one of them had a cell phone to call for help. But would they be reached in time?

This short video shows how TruePosition reenacted this story in order to demonstrate the advantages their U-TDOA technology has over other location technologies. With U-TDOA, the men would have been located in just 15 minutes instead of the almost 4 hours it actually took.
